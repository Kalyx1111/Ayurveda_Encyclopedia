VATA — Air + Space

Qualities

  • Dry, Light, Cold, Rough, Mobile

Governs

  • All movement, breathing, nerve impulses, elimination, creativity

Seat

  • Colon, skin, ears, bones, pelvic region

Imbalance Signs

  • Anxiety, constipation, dry skin, insomnia, joint cracking, gas, irregular digestion, underweight

Balance With

  • Warm, oily, heavy foods · Regular routine · Sesame oil massage · Rest · Ginger, Ashwagandha

PITTA — Fire + Water

Qualities

  • Hot, Sharp, Light, Oily, Liquid

Governs

  • Digestion, metabolism, intelligence, vision, body temperature, skin colour

Seat

  • Small intestine, liver, spleen, blood, skin, eyes

Imbalance Signs

  • Acidity, inflammation, anger, rashes, acne, hair loss, diarrhoea, fever, perfectionism

Balance With

  • Cool, sweet, bitter foods · Coconut, ghee, coriander · Cooling activities · Shatavari, Amalaki

KAPHA — Earth + Water

Qualities

  • Heavy, Slow, Cold, Oily, Smooth, Dense, Stable

Governs

  • Structure, lubrication, immunity, growth, strength, memory, love

Seat

  • Chest, lungs, stomach, joints, head, sinuses

Imbalance Signs

  • Weight gain, congestion, lethargy, depression, diabetes, attachment, oversleeping, slow digestion

Balance With

  • Light, dry, warm, spicy foods · Vigorous exercise · Honey, ginger, pepper · Trikatu, Guggulu

Panchakarma — Five Purifications

  • Vamana (Therapeutic emesis) — Removes Kapha from stomach/chest. For: asthma, sinusitis, psoriasis, obesity. Requires 3-7 days Purvakarma first.
  • Virechana (Therapeutic purgation) — Removes Pitta from small intestine/liver. Most commonly performed. For: skin diseases, acidity, liver disorders, headaches.
  • Basti (Medicated enema) — Most important of five ("Ardha Chikitsa"). Removes Vata from colon. For: all Vata disorders, arthritis, constipation, neurological, infertility.
  • Nasya (Nasal therapy) — Removes Kapha/Vata from head. For: migraine, sinusitis, hair loss, brain disorders, cervical spondylosis.
  • Raktamokshana (Bloodletting) — Removes vitiated Rakta/Pitta. Methods: Leech therapy (Jalauka), venesection. For: skin diseases, gout, varicose veins.

⚠️ Panchakarma must ONLY be done under qualified supervision. Never self-administer.

Seasonal Routine (Ritucharya)

  • Vasanta (Spring, Feb-Apr): Kapha melts — allergies, congestion. Light, bitter, dry diet. Vamana season. Honey water, ginger, barley. Exercise vigorously.
  • Grishma (Summer, May-Jul): Pitta rises. Cool, sweet, hydrating foods. Coconut water, sweet fruits, mint. Avoid salt and heat. Rest midday. Moonlit walks.
  • Varsha (Monsoon, Jul-Sep): Vata rises, Agni weakest. Light digestible food. Boil water. Basti season. Ginger, ghee, old grains. Avoid raw foods.
  • Sharad (Autumn, Sep-Nov): Pitta erupts from summer. Virechana season. Sweet, bitter, astringent diet. Amla excellent. Avoid curd, fish. Moon baths.
  • Hemanta (Early Winter, Nov-Jan): Agni strongest. Build Ojas. Heavy nourishing diet. Milk, ghee, sesame, vigorous exercise. Best time for Rasayana.
  • Shishira (Late Winter, Jan-Feb): Transition. Warm nourishing food. Daily oil massage. Begin lightening as Kapha builds toward spring.

Tongue & Stool Diagnosis

Tongue Diagnosis

  • Healthy tongue: Pink-red, moist, no coating, slightly pointed
  • White thick coating: Ama (toxins) — clear Agni first
  • Yellow coating: Pitta excess, liver/bile issue
  • Brown/black coating: Severe Vata/Ama; chronic condition
  • Cracks: Chronic Vata, dehydration, nutritional deficiency
  • Red tip: Heart Pitta, emotional stress
  • Scalloped edges: Kapha, malabsorption, dental impressions

Stool Diagnosis (Mala Pariksha)

  • Healthy: Brown, formed, floats, passed easily, minimal odour, once daily in morning
  • Hard, dry, difficult: Vata — constipation, add ghee, warm water, Triphala
  • Loose, yellow, burning: Pitta — diarrhoea, inflammation, add Kutaj
  • Pale, mucus-coated, heavy: Kapha — Ama, malabsorption, add Trikatu

Swastha Lakshana — Signs of Perfect Health

According to Sushruta Samhita (15.41), a perfectly healthy person (Swastha) exhibits:

  • Sama Dosha — Balanced Vata, Pitta, Kapha
  • Sama Agni — Strong, regular, balanced digestive fire
  • Sama Dhatu — Seven tissues in optimal state
  • Mala Kriya — Proper elimination of all waste products
  • Prasanna Atma — Clear, peaceful, happy consciousness
  • Prasanna Mana — Calm, focused, undisturbed mind
  • Prasanna Indriya — Healthy, clear sense organs

Ayu (Lifespan) Factors

  • Ahara (Food): Fresh, warm, wholesome, appropriate
  • Nidra (Sleep): Regular 6-8hrs, before 10pm
  • Brahmacharya: Moderation in all sensory pleasures
  • Vyayama (Exercise): Daily, to half capacity
  • Saucha (Cleanliness): Internal and external purity
  • Sattva (Virtuous conduct): Ethical, compassionate living
